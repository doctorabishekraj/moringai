Our Story

Growing from the
roots of India

Moringai was born from a simple conviction: India's most ancient superfood deserved a world-class platform. Our founders, raised in the moringa-rich villages of Tamil Nadu, watched this miraculous tree — the "Tree of Life" — be overlooked by modern wellness markets.

We set out to change that. By building direct relationships with organic farmers, establishing rigorous quality standards, and creating beautiful, honest products, we've grown Moringai into India's most trusted moringa brand.

2021

Founded in Puducherry

Started with 12 farming families and a single moringa powder product.

2022

First Lab Certification

Received NABL lab certification. Expanded to 5 products and 50+ farmers.

2023

Nationwide Distribution

Pan-India shipping launched. Crossed 10,000 customers milestone.

2025

50,000 Happy Customers

200+ farming families, 18 products, and a growing community of wellness seekers.

Taste the Difference

Try Moringai risk-free. 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all products.
